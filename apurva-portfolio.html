My Story

I'm a Product Manager working at the intersection of HealthTech, AI, and User Experience. With a background in software QA and data analytics, I specialize in turning complex, ambiguous problems into intuitive products that create measurable impact. My passion was sparked by seeing how modern AI tools can fundamentally improve operability in healthcare — and that's the mission I'm building toward.

What I Do

I lead 0-to-1 product development — from market research and competitive analysis through PRDs, roadmaps, and launch. I thrive in fast-moving environments where PM, design, and engineering intersect.

Current Focus

Building practical AI-powered products at AdviNOW that scale. Actively exploring how GenAI can simplify clinical workflows and reduce friction for both patients and providers.
